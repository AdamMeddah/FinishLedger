Workflow

Keep job records accurate from entry to analysis.

Add an Order

  1. Open the Dashboard and choose Add.
  2. Enter the client, service type, revenue, cost, and order date.
  3. Submit the form. The dashboard updates without a page refresh.

Update an Order

  1. Choose Update and enter the invoice ID.
  2. Load the existing record, edit the fields, and save.
  3. Use the Order Log to confirm the corrected record.

Review Trends

  1. Open Insights to compare cumulative profit and service profitability.
  2. Hover on chart marks for invoice and profit details.
  3. Use these charts to replace recurring spreadsheet summaries.
